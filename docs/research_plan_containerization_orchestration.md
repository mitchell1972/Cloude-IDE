# Research Plan: Comprehensive Containerization and Orchestration Strategy for Cloud-Based IDE Platform

## Overview
This research plan outlines the systematic approach to design a comprehensive containerization and orchestration strategy for a Cloud-Based IDE platform. The strategy will incorporate security, scalability, multi-tenancy, and cost optimization based on existing research findings.

## Research Objectives
Design a production-ready containerization and orchestration solution that addresses:
- Secure multi-tenant code execution environments
- Scalable infrastructure supporting thousands of concurrent users
- Cost-effective resource management
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance
- Real-time collaborative development features
